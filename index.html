Our Products & Solutions

Technical products for professional applicators, contractors and building owners. Below is a summary of our core product groups.

Prefabricated Membranes (APP)

Application: terraces, podiums, basements.

  • Economical, easy to apply
  • Excellent adhesion and temperature flexibility
  • Suitable for horizontal and vertical surfaces

Self-Adhesive Membranes (SBS)

Products: Rapidbric HD & Rapidbric M — 1.5 mm

  • Cold-applied — no heating, safe & low pollution
  • Highly flexible; ideal for terraces, podiums, basements
  • Quick, economical installation; good tear & puncture resistance

Pre-applied Membranes

Innovative solutions for raft & basement waterproofing

  • Pre-applied fully bonded sheet membranes (SBS, HDPE)
  • Pre-applied SBS bituminous self-adhesive membranes
  • Ideal for basements and retaining walls

Liquid Applied Membranes (LAM)

Seamless, monolithic waterproofing for complex details

  • Excellent adhesion to varied substrates
  • Flexible systems for balconies, terraces and wet areas

Cementitious Waterproof Coatings

Easy-to-apply coatings for tanks, basements and water retaining structures.

Waterproofing Additives

Chemical admixtures and additives to enhance durability and performance.

Thermal Insulation (XPS)

XPS Technonicol CARBON PROF 300 — closed-cell extruded polystyrene boards.

  • High thermal insulation and mechanical compression resistance
  • Thickness options: 25 mm, 50 mm, 75 mm
  • Performance warranty up to 10 years (depending on thickness)

Acoustic Insulation

Noise barrier solutions for walls, ceilings and floors; polyester panels for interior applications.

Roofing — Bituminous Shingles

Tegola shingle lines, Master J, Top Shingle — multiple profiles & colours.

Special product: Acti Roof — photo-catalytic shingles that convert pollutants to harmless salts, are self-cleaning and discourage algae growth.

  • Acti Roof AR GREEN — 100 cm x 34 cm, 3.1 mm, 125 GSM fiberglass reinforcement
  • Shingles per bundle and weight details available on request

Stone-coated Metal Tiles

Lightweight, aesthetic roofing options with varied profiles and colours.

FAQs

Which waterproofing system is best for terraces?

Self-adhesive SBS membranes (Rapidbric HD) or APP membranes are commonly used — choice depends on substrate, exposure and budget. We provide site assessment and recommend the right system.
